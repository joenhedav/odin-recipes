Easy Homemade Pizza

Description

Make this homemade pizza with basic ingredients, then top it with any of your favorite toppings

Ingredients

  • 1 envelope active dry yeast
  • Lukewarm water
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Tomato paste
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Make the dough
  3. Make the sauce
  4. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  5. Cover with sauce and mozzarella cheese
  6. Bake until crust are golden brown, about 20 minutes
